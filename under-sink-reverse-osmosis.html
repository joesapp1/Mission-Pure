RO questions we answer daily

Clear answers for Dallas-Fort Worth kitchens.
What does reverse osmosis remove?
RO takes out dissolved solids (TDS), PFAS indicators, lead, arsenic, nitrates, fluoride, and microplastics when paired with catalytic carbon.
Does RO waste water?
Modern systems use efficient membranes (~2:1). We can add permeate pumps to cut waste further.
Can you tie RO into my fridge or ice maker?
Yes. We run dedicated lines to refrigerators, coffee machines, or pot fillers so every source pours RO water.
How often do filters change?
Carbon + sediment stages change every 12 months, membranes every 24–36 months depending on water usage and TDS.
