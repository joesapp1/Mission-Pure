Drinking Water

Reverse Osmosis Systems

Under-sink RO units that strip PFAS, lead, chromium-6, nitrates, and dissolved solids down to near-zero — delivering purified, great-tasting water directly from a dedicated faucet at your sink.

Micromax™ 8500
Flagship 5-stage under-sink reverse osmosis system. Sediment pre-filter, catalytic carbon, RO membrane, post-carbon polish, and a 3.2-gallon tank. Handles high TDS, PFAS indicators, chromium-6, and lead to near-zero detection levels.
Micromax™ 7000
4-stage compact RO designed for tighter under-sink cabinets. Ideal for new construction or when space is limited. Same high-rejection membrane as the 8500 in a slimmer footprint.
Pur-Alkaline Filter
Optional remineralization stage added after the RO membrane. Re-introduces calcium and magnesium for naturally balanced pH, better taste, and minerals your body actually uses.
