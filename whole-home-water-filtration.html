Whole-home FAQ

Answers to our most common calls.
Will a whole-home filter slow my water pressure?
We size media tanks and control valves to match your home’s demand. Most systems maintain or improve pressure thanks to clean plumbing.
Do I still need under-sink RO?
Whole-home filtration protects showers, laundry, and appliances. Add RO for drinking/cooking so dissolved solids and PFAS drop to near-zero.
How often does the media change?
Catalytic carbon typically lasts 5–6 years; softener resin lasts even longer. Our Care Plan tracks usage and schedules proactive swaps.
Can you install in tight garages or outdoors?
Yes. We mount in garages, side yards, or mechanical rooms with UV-rated jackets and insulated bypasses where needed.
