## Core talking points
1. **Real-time contaminant intel** – Water Watch publishes PFAS/TTHM alerts and install data ahead of official notices, so homeowners act before taste/odor complaints explode on Facebook groups.
2. **ZIP-based lookup** – Residents enter a ZIP, pick their utility, and see contaminant multipliers (PFAS, lead, chromium-6, chloramine, hardness, VOCs).
3. **Same-week installs** – Mission Pure can install catalytic carbon + softening systems and under-sink RO within 3–5 days (faster for RO-only emergencies).
4. **Community education** – Available for HOA meetings, moms groups, and neighborhood forums with live contaminant reports tailored to their ZIPs.
5. **Local proof** – Latest highlights: Arlington TTHMs 2.6× guideline (media swap case), Plano moms adopting RO for baby prep, McKinney lead line bypass, Southlake chloramine relief.
