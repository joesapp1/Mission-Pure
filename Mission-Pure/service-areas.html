Service area FAQ

Still unsure if we reach your neighborhood? Start here.
Do you work outside Dallas-Fort Worth?
Our core team focuses on DFW. If you’re just outside (e.g., Waco, Sherman), reach out—we may coordinate a partner install.
What if my ZIP isn’t in the lookup yet?
Send us your water bill or utility name. We’ll add it, pull data manually, and email you the report.
Do you coordinate with each city’s utility?
Yes. Mission Pure manages permits, backflow paperwork, and shutoffs with DWU, NTMWD cities, Upper Trinity, and local districts.
How fast can you schedule installs?
Most cities see availability within 3–5 days; urgent RO installs can often happen next-day.
