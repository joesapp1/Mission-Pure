## Questions & Answers
1. **Do you service the entire Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex?**  
   Yes. Mission Pure covers Dallas, Fort Worth, Frisco, Plano, McKinney, Allen, Garland, Irving, Arlington, Denton, Rockwall, Southlake, Grapevine, Royse City, Fate, and surrounding suburbs. Use the ZIP lookup on our site and we’ll pull your utility data.

2. **How fast can you install a whole-home filter after I call?**  
   Most installs are scheduled within 3–5 days. Urgent under-sink RO upgrades can often be completed next-day once we confirm your utility and plumbing layout.

3. **What contaminants do your systems remove?**  
   We target PFAS, lead, chromium-6, chloramine byproducts (TTHMs, HAA5), VOCs, and hardness. Each quote includes a ZIP/utility lookup so we match filtration media to your exact water report.

4. **Do you offer financing or memberships?**  
   Yes. Mission Pure offers flexible financing plus the Mission Pure Care Plan for ongoing media changes, salt delivery, and priority service.

5. **Can you check my water if my ZIP isn’t in your tool yet?**  
   Send us your water bill or utility name. We’ll add the ZIP, pull state or EPA data manually, and email you the report with recommendations.

6. **Will a whole-home system hurt my water pressure?**  
   No. We size tanks and control valves to your home’s peak demand and verify pressure at install. Most customers report equal or better pressure after scale and buildup are removed.
